Inception Protocol

Synopsis

In a near-future where consciousness can be digitized and transferred between vessels, a rogue detective is tasked with hunting down a 'Ghost Protocol'—a digital entity that has breached the securest neural network in the city. As the lines between physical reality and digital simulation blur, he discovers that his own memories may be part of the infiltration.
